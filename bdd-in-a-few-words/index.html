BDD in a few words

BDD is primarily a conceptual approach also knows as Specification by Example.
It is a Collaboration Technic where all the actors of the team meet together in a meeting called “The 3 Amigos” to design stories related to a feature in the form of acceptance scenarios.

During the “The 3 Amigos” meeting, conversations take place between the business analyst, the developer and the tester and they elaborate the requirements together.

The output of this meeting is a set of acceptance scenarios corresponding to the requirements they have been discussing about.

These scenarios guide the developer and act as automated tests.
These are very well suited to work in relation with Cucumber automation testing tool.

Below is an example of a very simple acceptance scenario :

Story: Returns go to stock
As a store owner
In order to keep track of stock
I want to add items back to stock when they're returned.


Scenario 1: Refunded items should be returned to stock
Given that a customer previously bought a black sweater from me
And I have three black sweaters in stock.
When he returns the black sweater for a refund
Then I should have four black sweaters in stock.


Scenario 2: Replaced items should be returned to stock
Given that a customer previously bought a blue garment from me
And I have two blue garments in stock
And three black garments in stock.
When he returns the blue garment for a replacement in black
Then I should have three blue garments in stock
And two black garments in stock.
